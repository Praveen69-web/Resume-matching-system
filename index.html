PDF Parsing

Extracts raw text from every page using Mozilla PDF.js — entirely in your browser, no server.

NLP Pipeline

Lowercase → Tokenize → Stopword removal → Term frequency. Real NLP in your browser.

TF-IDF Vectors

Text becomes weighted numeric vectors. Rare, meaningful terms score higher; filler words fade.

Cosine Similarity

dot(A,B)/(|A|×|B|) — angle between resume & job vector. Closer = stronger semantic match.
